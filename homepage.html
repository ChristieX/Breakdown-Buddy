~~RELIABLE HELP FOR UNRELIABLE BREAKDOWNS

Breakdowns can lead to significant delays, inconvenience, and safety concerns for drivers and other road users.

An on-road vehicle breakdown application can be a game-changer, providing drivers with a reliable and convenient way to request for assistance and receive quick and efficient support

REQUEST ASSISTANCE

Allows drivers to request immediate assistance during a breakdown or emergency situation.

ASSISTANCE GUIDES

Offers guidance and support to drivers for resolving common vehicle issues, such as changing a flat tire or jumpstarting a car.

EMERGENCY CONTACTS

Provides a list of emergency contacts, such as towing companies and nearby repair shops, in case the breakdown assistant is unable to resolve the issue.
